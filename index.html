Who is this for?

Start Small is designed for students taking algorithms courses, especially those who are new to:

  • Recursion
  • Dynamic Programming
  • Greedy Strategies

If you've ever felt stuck at the first step of solving an algorithm problem, this blog is for you. We take a strategy-first approach, starting with small examples and building up to complete solutions. Our goal is to help you develop the thinking skills needed to tackle complex problems with confidence.
