Strategies and Takeaways

A systematic approach to solving algorithm problems

My Key Takeaways

What are algorithms? Algorithms are ways to create a series of repeatable actions that you can predictably take for any instance of a problem and get an expected output.

Start Small: Always begin with small examples and work your way up. This approach helps break down complex problems into manageable pieces and reveals patterns that might not be obvious at first glance.

Problem-Solving Strategy

Typically when I am approached with a new Algorithms problem to solve I work through a series of steps. All of these steps stem from one very key first step: always start with working through a small example (or a few) from there you have a few different ways to strategize your thinking.
