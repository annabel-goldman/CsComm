Strategy Toolbox

My Go-To Strategies for Tackling Algorithm Problems

A portable mental framework you can bring to any new problem

Start Small

Try solving with a tiny input by hand. This helps build intuition and reveals patterns.

Base Case Analysis

Think through the simplest version of the problem - what's the base case?

Recursive Thinking

How do you reduce a big problem to a smaller one? Break it down step by step.

Map Your Search Space

What choices do you have at each step? Visualize the decision tree.

Identify Waste

What work are you repeating or redoing? Look for opportunities to optimize.

Stay Calm

Don't panic - even if you don't see the full solution, partial ideas often reveal patterns.
